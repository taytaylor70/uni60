## 3 Rules to Never Break

1. **One primary keyword per page.** Don't dilute. Each page owns one term and supports it with 2–3 related phrases naturally in the body copy.

2. **Title tags are for Google. Headlines are for humans.** Never make them the same sentence. Your H1 (`The ID system your infrastructure deserves`) and your title tag (`UUID Alternative for Python — HEXAGEN-60 Protocol`) serve different audiences.

3. **The Intelligence section is your compound asset.** Every article you publish there earns authority that lifts every other page. Write for senior engineers, not for search engines — Google rewards it the same way.
